Our Story

Marietta Restaurant was founded with a simple idea — to bring people together over fresh, high-quality food in the heart of Marietta, Ohio.

What started as a small family-inspired kitchen has grown into a welcoming local restaurant where friends, families, and visitors can enjoy meals made with care and consistency.

We believe that great food doesn’t need to be complicated. It just needs to be fresh, flavorful, and served in a place where people feel at home.

“Our goal is simple: serve food we’re proud of in a place our community loves to gather.”

What We Stand For

At Marietta Restaurant, everything we do is guided by three core values:

  • Fresh Ingredients: We prepare meals daily using quality ingredients.
  • Community First: We are proud to serve Marietta and surrounding areas.
  • Simple, Honest Food: No shortcuts—just great meals made right.

A Local Gathering Place

Located in downtown Marietta, Ohio, our restaurant has become a place where people come together—whether it’s for a casual lunch, a family dinner, or a night out with friends.

We proudly serve guests from Marietta, Belpre, Parkersburg, and throughout Washington County.
